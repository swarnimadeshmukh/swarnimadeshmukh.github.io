About Me

I’m a user-experience designer with a knack for balancing form and function in digital interfaces. I’m currently wrapping up my MS degree at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, specializing in Human Computer Interaction. I’m also a part-time User Experience Designer at FICO, Ann Arbor.

I love working with enterprise applications that deal with a large amount of data and building intuitive and seamless experiences for users. I strongly believe in and practise user-centered design, which is why research and usability testing are critical components of my design process. For me, it’s all in the details!

As soon as I completed my undergraduate degree in engineering, I joined Microsoft in Hyderabad, India as a Software Engineer 1. I was given an opportunity to spend the next 2 years rotating in 4 different teams, to get a breadth of experience valuable in the nascent stages of my career. In the first team itself, I worked on the user-interface and interaction design for a Lync 2013 application. This piqued my interest and I went on to design Windows Phone and Store applications for the next 2 teams. Soon enough, I moved to Ann Arbor, Michigan to dive deeper into the domain of interaction design in the form of a graduate degree. I joined FICO last summer as a user experience intern. I loved it so much that I stayed on as a part-time designer while continuing my education in Ann Arbor.

I’ve also worked as a designer and consultant at a design agency at the university which works with local startups and entrepreneurs. Apart from that, I’ve worked with several smaller organizations like BGM Engineering Inc, Arbor Insight, Jackson Interfaith Shelter, and Services to Enhance Potential!

Beyond work, I love to travel! I’ve explored quite a few places so far, but the list keeps growing! I also volunteer for local organizations from time to time. I worked with Make a Difference, a non-profit aimed at empowering children at risk, in Pune, India. As a part of social outreach in Microsoft, I taught basic computer skills to underprivileged children in Hyderabad, India. Recently, I helped out at the Ann Arbor District Library for MLK Day! When I’m indoors, I love weekends in my cocoon, binge watching Netflix!
