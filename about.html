About Me

I'm a user experience designer with a background in development, with a knack for balancing form and function in digital interfaces. I'm currently pursuing an MS desgree in Information, specializing in HCI, at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor.

I started working for Microsoft, India, immediately after my undergraduate degree, as part of the Microsoft Academy for College Hires program. I worked in 3 different teams, raking in a breadth of experience in design and agile software development. In my first rotation as part of Microsoft Business Solutions, I helped design the UI and navigation for a help-tool on Lync 2013 called 'LyncBot', which helped customers and partners quickly access FAQs. In my next rotation, I helped design a Windows 8.1 Phone app for sales leaders in Microsoft, which quickly fetched account information from CRM and enabled users to directly contact account managers. Following this, I worked on the design and implementation of a Windows 10 Desktop app intended to deliver a clean and intuitive user-interface for OEM partners to perform complex administrative tasks in CRM seamlessly.

Last summer, I worked for FICO as a UX Intern, to re-design the FICO Enterprise Security Score web application in order to ensure optimal user interaction. I'm still a part of the awesome FICO team in Ann Arbor, as a part-time designer, to hone the finer nuances of the application.
